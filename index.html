About Us

We're always visiting and revisiting places and discovering new favorite stops on our trips. Once we're back home, its easy to forget about these places till its time for our next trip, or till a friend asks for recommendations. Those days are in the past, Tripster is here to save the day (and your next trip!)


We hope you enjoy what we've put together and encourage you to recommend your favorite spots! We love to discover new hits!

NYC

New York
Why We Love it The city that never sleeps. If you cant find it in NYC, you cant find it anywhere. The bustling city has everything you need to see, eat, and buy. Know your way around, and how to stay out of New Yorkers' way, and you'll be set.

From Fifth Ave to Soho to LES, we've got you covered. Times Square and Broadway are must sees for first timers, and if the weather permits dont leave without taking a walk on the High Line.
